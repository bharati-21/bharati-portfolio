ABOUT ME

My Technical Journey

My journey with Computer Science began in secondary school, when I opted for Computer Science + PCM as my major stream for class 11th and 12th. What began as a path lacking direction, led to my love for teaching. Hence, I decided to go for Bachelor of Computer Applications for my graduation and Computer Science as my major

Discovering my Love for Programming

Halfway through my graduation degree, I started enjoying programming, and decided to explore the domain further. After participating in a few inter-collegiate Hackathons, I had a change of heart and took a bold leap from teaching to programming.

Thus began my ride of Software Development, Web Development, Android Application Development, and also Machine Learning.

After College, What's Next Now?

I graduated with a degree in B.C.A. in the year 2020. A major question that kept creeping back is what now, that I am done with college? I did get placed in a reputed Consulting US firm, but then I didn't know where to go now in terms on learning. I tried exploring Machine Learning and Data Science, but then I found myself in a road block.

That's when I discovered neoG.Camp in November 2020. I had a few more months to onboard into the firm, so I utilized that time in building and refactoring my skills. Not only did this initiative make me confident, they also gave me a proper vision and a guide for my future ahead.

What am I upto now?

I am constantly learning, evolving, exploring and developing.

Professionally, I have been working in Deloitte India (Offices of the U.S) as an Associate Analyst since 18th January 2021.

My Interests and Hobbies

My technical interests align with software and web development. I have also tried summoning my inner designer, but haven't been able to do so yet! Maybe someday in the future!?

Apart from tech, I love singing and reading books. I am an avid reader, and read almost everyday. My favorite genres are fiction, fantasy, thriller, adventure and mystery. I have also been trained in Classical Carnatic Music for over 12 years.
